Course: Generative AI with ComfyUI

Nov 18, 2025

The world of Generative AI is complex, and fast-moving. [...] There are so many models, and so many complexities, that this course was essential to cut through all the noise.

Matt Swoboda - Notch - Founder and Lead Developer

Over the last few years, we have seen a rapid and wide-spread uptake of generative AI in industry and academia. One tool has established itself as the de-facto standard for rapid experimentation, as well as professional production: ComfyUI. It is used in projects ranging from commercial productions like The Wizard of Oz at the Las Vegas Sphere up to leading-edge research projects like Bytedance’s HyperLORA.

Based on my personal experience with using ComfyUI almost daily from day 1, I have developed a course with unique features:

  • Only uses open-weights models that can be run on your own machine; no cloud services are used.
  • From the ground up: after 1.5 hours into this course, you will understand the underlying concept of every single item in the simplest ComfyUI workflow, including: Flow Matching, Variational Autoencoders, CLIP, and CFG.
  • Dozens of hand-crafted workflows as starting point for your own explorations.
  • Covers essential system administration tasks to allow continuous upgrading and also rollbacks of your ComfyUI installation.

The course contains theoretical presentations, demonstrations, and hands-on exercises. The main modules are:

  1. Course Introduction
  2. Image Generation
  3. Video Generation
  4. Complex Workflows; e.g.
    • Spatio-temporal upscaling of videos (e.g. 16fps@720p => 60fps@4k)
    • Powerful workflows combining vision language models, large language models, and video generation models

The full course takes 2 days but can be presented as a distilled version in 1 day. Typical class size is 8, but can be scaled from 1-20. The content will be adapted to the client’s needs, for example:

  • Technical vs. design focus
  • Audience size
  • Available hardware for students
